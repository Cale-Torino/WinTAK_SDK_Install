Detailed Description

Represents a position expressed in Earth Centered, Earth Fixed coordinates.

This Earth Centered, Earth Fixed coordinate system is using meters as its distance unit, and has the positive X axis coming out of the earth at 0 latitude, 0 longitude, the positive Y axis coming out in the indian ocean, and the positive Z axis coming out of the north pole
